## 6. Independent Contractor Status

Contractor is an independent contractor, not an employee. Contractor:
- Controls how and when they work, subject to agreed milestones and communication norms
- Is responsible for their own tax obligations and filings in their jurisdiction
- Is not entitled to employment benefits of any kind
- May work for other clients, provided it does not conflict with Latch obligations

Nothing in this Agreement creates a partnership, joint venture, or employment relationship.
